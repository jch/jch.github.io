Reducing Tailwind asset size

I consolidated the form and typography styling I was using across jch.app and reduced the stylesheet bundle size 56% from 156kb to 68k. There's more room to trim from the default reset and utility classes, but this felt like a good stopping place.

$ find public -name '*.css' | xargs du -sh | sort -n
